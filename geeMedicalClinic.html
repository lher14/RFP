about us

The Gees Wellness Clinic is a collaborative effort between Gee's Barbershop and Anthem, harnessing the trust and influence of barbershops within the African American community. This vital cultural institution offers a unique opportunity to bridge the gap between clients' needs and the wealth of health and social services available in Milwaukee. Our initiative seeks to meet men where they are and provide assistance to those seeking connections to appropriate primary and behavioral health care providers, as well as social service resources.

In this project, barbers play a pivotal role by referring their clients to a designated Wellness Genius, who conducts assessments and facilitates enrollment into the program. These Wellness Geniuses are members of the Anthem staff.

Cardiovascular

Getting your cardiovascular health checked is essential to assess your risk of heart disease and related conditions. Regular check-ups can help detect issues early, enabling timely interventions and lifestyle adjustments to maintain a healthy heart and prevent serious health problems.

Cholesterol

Checking your cholesterol levels is crucial as it allows you to monitor your risk of developing heart disease. Understanding your cholesterol profile helps in making informed dietary and lifestyle choices, reducing the likelihood of cholesterol-related complications like atherosclerosis and heart attacks.

Blood Pressure

Regularly monitoring your blood pressure is vital for maintaining overall health. It helps identify hypertension, a significant risk factor for heart disease and stroke, allowing for early intervention through medication or lifestyle changes to prevent serious cardiovascular issues.

why choose us

People should consider a free walk-in clinic because it offers accessible healthcare services without the burden of upfront costs, making it an affordable option for those without insurance or with limited financial resources. Additionally, walk-in clinics typically have shorter wait times compared to traditional healthcare settings, ensuring prompt care for minor illnesses and injuries.
